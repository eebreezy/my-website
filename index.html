Kids 3D Games Pack

This upgraded pack includes real browser-based 3D games using WebGL and Three.js. They are brighter, smoother, and much closer to the type of graphics people expect from modern kid-friendly web games.

What changed

Instead of 20 simple mini-games, this pack focuses on 5 better-looking 3D games with reusable structure, lighting, shadows, particles, score systems, and mobile-friendly controls. This is a much stronger base for publishing and expanding.

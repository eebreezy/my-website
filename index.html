Lesson Author (no code)

Create your own Rosetta‑style lessons. Save to browser, export JSON to share.

Task types:

  1. image-mc — pick the right picture for the prompt.
  2. phrase-mc — choose the correct matching phrase.
  3. arrange — build the sentence from word tiles.
  4. speak — say the sentence (speech recognition if available).
  5. listen-mc — hear audio, pick the text you heard.

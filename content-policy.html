Content Policy

OmFunny is a community for sharing memes. To keep it safe and fun, the following rules apply:

Allowed Content

  • Funny and entertaining images
  • Original memes
  • Lighthearted content

Not Allowed

  • Illegal content of any kind
  • Sexual or explicit content
  • Child exploitation (will be reported immediately)
  • Violence or graphic imagery
  • Hate speech or harassment
  • Copyrighted content you do not own
  • Spam or misleading content

Moderation

All uploads may be reviewed before appearing publicly. We may remove content without notice.

Reporting

If you see something that violates this policy, report it immediately.

Violations may result in content removal or account suspension.
